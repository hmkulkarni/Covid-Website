About

We at "Gift a Life" intend what exactly is said in the name - Giving the gift of life to those in need. Amidst COVID-19 situation, many people are losing hope. Some of them cannot get adequate life supplies, while some lose their entire family.

We strive to give their life a meaning, by connecting you with those who are in need. Many patients who are in critical condition due to COVID are in need of Plasma. Let us reach out to them through the means of the people viewing and accessing this website at this moment.

Many children are stranded due to loss of their loved ones. They are all alone by themselves with no one to care for. We bring the opportunity to connect you with the orphanages, when you're willing to adopt a child. Give them your love, they will give back a thousand times more to you.
